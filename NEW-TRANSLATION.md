## NOTES FOR TRANSLATORS

1. **Placeholders:** Strings with `{{price}}`, `{{n}}`, `{{date}}` contain dynamic values. Preserve the placeholder format.
2. **Brand names:** "Japam", "AI Developer India" may be kept as-is or transliterated per language.
3. **Deity names:** Rama, Shiva, Ganesh, etc. are typically transliterated, not translated.
4. **Episode names:** Veda Bhoomi, Upanishad Vanam, etc. are Sanskrit/sanathana terms—transliterate or translate as appropriate.
5. **Legal text:** Privacy Policy, Terms, Refund, Shipping pages contain long blocks—translate fully for compliance.
6. **HTML meta:** Title and description in index.html affect SEO and link previews—translate for each locale.
